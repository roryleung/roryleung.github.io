🎮 How PhDone works

💷 Guaranteed rewards: Completing a task always adds the reward you chose beforehand. Randomness only affects optional bonuses.

🔥 Weekly ranks: Weekly XP resets every Monday, keeping the next goal close. Each rank has a one-time claimable reward for that week.

🎲 Daily quests: Three quests rotate each day. Finish and claim them for XP, money or mystery boxes.

🗺️ Weekly challenge: A larger goal refreshes each Monday and gives a mixed reward.

🎁 Mystery boxes: Every box wins something. Results never remove money or XP.

🏛️ Lifetime milestones: These remain as a quiet record of long-term progress, not an ever-receding main target.

🏛️ Lifetime milestones

Your lifetime XP never resets. It records everything you have earned through completed tasks, claimed habits and bonus XP.

Milestone bonuses are claimable once. They add spending money but do not create more XP, so they cannot trigger a chain of automatic level-ups.

The weekly rank is the main game. Lifetime levels are simply a scrapbook of sustained progress.
